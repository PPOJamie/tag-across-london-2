Quick reference

Rules at a glance

Open full rulesheet
Round flow
  1. Roll an unused start, then a valid unused destination for that start.
  2. Runners receive a 15-minute head start.
  3. Runners touch both station signs and send photo proof to connect the route.
  4. A Chaser catches a Runner by physically touching them before the route is connected; use the selected catch-scoring setting.
  5. Record the outcome, switch roles and begin the next round.
Movement
  • Walking is allowed at any time.
  • Taxis, private couriers and the London Eye are banned.
  • A bus may be used for no more than 10 minutes, with no immediate bus-to-bus transfer.
  • Do not split up except within an enclosed space.
Station updates
  • On leaving or entering a station, message the Chasers with the station and time.
  • To change trains, exit the station and spin a challenge.
  • Getting back on exactly the same train is permitted without a challenge.
Challenges
  • Proof is required.
  • One free reroll is allowed per round.
  • After 15 minutes without completion, a new challenge may be rolled.
  • Challenges already completed during the game are automatically excluded from future spins.
Winning
  • The team with the most points wins.
  • If points are tied, the team that completed the most challenges wins.
Safety
  • Obey transport staff, road rules and local laws.
  • Do not trespass or obstruct the public.
  • Use the safety override for anything unsafe, inaccessible or inappropriate in the moment.
